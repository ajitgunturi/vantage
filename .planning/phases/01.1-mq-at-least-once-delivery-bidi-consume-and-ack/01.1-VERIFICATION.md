## Deviations & Notes

- **Park-select ack bug found & fixed during execution:** the RESEARCH Pattern 1 empty-store park discarded an arriving ack (`case <-ackCh:` consumes the value), stalling `Consumed`. Fixed to process the ack inline (delete lease, consumed++, replenish token). Captured in 01.1-03-SUMMARY decisions.
- **`Shutdown()` semantics:** consumers now terminate on stream-context cancellation (gRPC GracefulStop), not a shutdown channel; `Shutdown()` is retained idempotent for cmd/mq ordering. Reframed test `TestMQServer_Shutdown_ReturnsOnDisconnect`.
