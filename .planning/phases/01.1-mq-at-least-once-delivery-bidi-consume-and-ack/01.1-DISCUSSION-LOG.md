## Origin — reproduced defect

Produce 1000, then `mqprobe -n 20 -mode consume`: client read 20 but
`consumed_total` reached 513, and a follow-up drain recovered only ~399 of the
rest — ~493 messages silently lost. Root cause: eager dispatch drains the ring
into `workCh` (cap 1024) and `stream.Send` buffers into gRPC's flow-control
window; in-flight messages die on consumer disconnect (at-most-once).
