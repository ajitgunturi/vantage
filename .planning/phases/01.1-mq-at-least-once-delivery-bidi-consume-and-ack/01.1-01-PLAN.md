<objective>
Upgrade the MQ proto contract to support broker-side at-least-once delivery (MQ-09) and
client-driven credit flow control (MQ-10), then regenerate the Go stubs in `pkg/pb`.

This is the contract-first foundation: the bidirectional `Consume` signature, the broker
message id, and the `ConsumeClientMsg` client->server message must exist as generated Go
types before the engine rewrite (Wave 2) and the bidi client (Wave 3) can compile.

annotations, PROJECT.md Key Decisions, REQUIREMENTS.md MQ-09/MQ-10 are all committed) — this
plan implements the contract, it does not re-document the decision.

Purpose: deliver the wire contract every other plan in the phase depends on.
Output: regenerated `pkg/pb/mq.pb.go` + `pkg/pb/mq_grpc.pb.go` with bidi Consume, `TelemetryMessage.Id`, and `ConsumeClientMsg`.
</objective>
