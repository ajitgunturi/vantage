<objective>
Rewrite the `mqprobe` smoke client to speak the new bidi at-least-once protocol (MQ-09, MQ-10):
open the bidi stream, send initial credit, then receive each message and ack it by broker id.
This replaces the old server-stream "drain without ack" that made `-mode consume` a misleading
peek. The probe is a gRPC client, so it depends only on the regenerated proto (Plan 01) and the
running engine semantics (Plan 03) — not on the inspect handler — and runs parallel with Plan 04.

Purpose: give the smoke suite (Plan 06) a probe that exercises the real bidi+ack wire path.
Output: bidi `mqprobe` with a `-credit` flag and ack-on-receive consume.
</objective>
